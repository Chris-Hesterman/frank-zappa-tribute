Frank Zappa

(1940-1993)

Musician, Author, Composer, Activist

A man of many talents, Frank Zappa's career in music spanned more than 30 years. From Rock to Jazz to Orchestral, he was quite prolific, producing 62 records in his lifetime, and 47 posthumously. Known for his unique style of guitar playing, his humor, and his political satire, Zappa's music was never easily categorized but always entertaining. He also produced a number of movies and videos.
